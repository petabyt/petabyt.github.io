The world has failed you and also hates you

April 26 2026

Let's recap the life of the average zoomer, from my perspective as an 21yo American:

  • Spend your youngest years playing Wii, Minecraft and nerf wars with friends
  • You live in a suburb with helicopter parents who don't let you do stuff alone
  • Time of your life in 2016-2017 with fidget spinners, water bottle flips, dank memes
  • Social media pressure and cyberbullying in middle school
  • Spend your formative high school years in quarantine with 0 social interation
  • Graduate into a frozen job market during the AI bubble

Okay, now imagine you're 18-24 years old and trying to imagine a future for yourself. What do you see out there?

A world without promises or prosperity

  • Wage growth is 140%, real estate growth is 400%
  • College is too expensive
    • Only 53% of students believe they can finance college... (Schwartz, 2023).
  • Companies are using AI to filter through resume submissions
  • Applicants use AI to submit resumes and talk to recruiters
  • People have anxiety over AI replacing jobs
  • 2026 has the the worst performing job market since 2008
  • Stable career, marriage, home ownership, and starting a family seems too unrealistic for most zoomers

Incompetent government that hates you

  • US national debt is growing faster than the economy
  • Pointless wars and wreckless spending while the next generation is struggling to see a future for themselves
  • The government is incompetent, stupid, and divided more than ever
